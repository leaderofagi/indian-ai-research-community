## Discussion Platforms

### MCIAIR Alpha — [mciair.in/hc.html](https://mciair.in/hc.html)
An HN-style discussion board for Indian AI research discourse. Features:
- Technical deep dives on agentic AI, LLMs, scaling laws, AI infrastructure
- Peer discussion with thoughtful critique
- Focus on India's AI research ecosystem and sovereign infrastructure
- Free & open — no paywall, no ads

### Hacker News — [news.ycombinator.com](https://news.ycombinator.com)
Global tech/startup/AI discussion. Indian researchers are active in threads about ML/AI papers.

### Lobsters — [lobste.rs](https://lobste.rs)
Tech-focused link aggregation with strong commenting culture.

## Academic Mailing Lists & Groups

### AI in India (Google Group) — [groups.google.com/g/ai-in-india](https://groups.google.com/g/ai-in-india)
~500 members. Active since 2010. CFP announcements, workshop notices, AI research ecosystem discussions. Moderated by Dr. Biplav Srivastava.

### Machine Learning News (Google Group) — [groups.google.com/g/ML-news](https://groups.google.com/g/ML-news)
IMLS-moderated mailing list for ML events, CFPs, and announcements.

### ACM India — [india.acm.org](https://india.acm.org/)
Hosts summer/winter schools, conferences, and research awards for Indian computing researchers.

### IndiaBioscience — [indiabioscience.org](https://indiabioscience.org/)
News, jobs, and community for life sciences and interdisciplinary researchers in India.
